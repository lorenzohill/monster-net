Getting Started



Lets get started!

So you want to be a hunter? Well you have came to the right place. There are a lot of monsters that roam around the earth. You just need to know how to look for them. Each of them have a weakness that you need to discover. Once you have found that out. You can take out the monster. Careful, some of these monsters can be very dangerious

Here are some tips:

  1. Gather Clues
  2. Inteview People
  3. Do Research
  4. Discover the monsters weakness
  5. Take care of the monster by any means necessary
